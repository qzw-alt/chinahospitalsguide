Plastic Surgery in China 2026: Liposuction, Rhinoplasty & Top Hospitals

World-class cosmetic procedures at 50-70% less than US prices — with top-rated hospitals in Beijing, Shanghai & Guangzhou

📅 March 15, 2026 (Updated July 17, 2026) ⏱️ 15 min read 🏥 Plastic Surgery

China has emerged as a global hub for cosmetic surgery, combining world-class medical expertise with advanced technology at prices significantly lower than Western countries. With highly skilled surgeons trained in both Eastern and Western techniques, Chinese plastic surgery centers offer natural-looking results tailored to diverse aesthetic preferences. This guide covers the top-ranked hospitals, procedure costs, and what to expect — with special focus on Beijing liposuction and the most sought-after procedures for international patients in 2026.

🏥 Top Plastic Surgery Hospitals in China

1. Shanghai Ninth People's Hospital (上海第九人民医院)

Location: Shanghai | Ranking: #1 in China for Plastic Surgery

Most prestigious plastic surgery department in China. Pioneers in Asian facial aesthetic surgery with comprehensive reconstructive and cosmetic services. Over 100,000 cosmetic procedures annually. Strong international patient department with English-speaking coordinators.

Best for: Complex rhinoplasty, facial contouring, reconstructive surgery

2. Peking Union Medical College Hospital (PUMCH / 北京协和医院)

Location: Beijing | Ranking: Top 3 nationally

Highest medical standards in China with comprehensive pre-operative assessment and excellent safety record. Board-certified plastic surgeons with international training. Strong reputation for liposuction, breast surgery, and post-weight-loss body contouring.

Best for: Liposuction, breast augmentation, body contouring, safety-focused patients

3. Plastic Surgery Hospital of CAMS (中国医学科学院整形外科医院)

Location: Beijing | Ranking: Top specialized plastic surgery center

China's only hospital fully dedicated to plastic and reconstructive surgery. Affiliated with the Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences. Specializes in the full spectrum of cosmetic procedures with a research-driven approach. Highly regarded for liposuction and body sculpting expertise.

Best for: Specialized liposuction, body contouring, complex cosmetic cases

📍 Beijing Liposuction: Top Hospitals & What to Expect

Beijing has become one of China's most sought-after destinations for liposuction and body contouring, with several top-tier hospitals offering world-class care at 60-65% less than US prices. Here's what international patients need to know about getting liposuction in Beijing in 2026.

Top-Rated Liposuction Hospitals in Beijing

Peking Union Medical College Hospital (PUMCH) — Best overall for safety and surgeon credentials. Board-certified plastic surgeons with international fellowships. Comprehensive pre-op assessment and post-op care. Prices: $2,000-$3,000 per area.

Plastic Surgery Hospital of CAMS — Specialized liposuction center with the highest procedure volume in Beijing. Offers traditional, ultrasound-assisted (UAL), and laser-assisted liposuction. Prices: $1,500-$2,500 per area.

Beijing United Family Hospital (北京和睦家医院) — Best for international patients seeking English-speaking care and Western-style service standards. JCI-accredited. Higher price point ($2,500-$3,500/area) but includes dedicated interpreter and international patient coordinator.

💡 Beijing Liposuction Quick Facts

  • Cost: $1,500-$3,000 per area (vs $4,000-$8,000 in the US)
  • Techniques available: Tumescent, ultrasound-assisted (UAL/VASER), laser-assisted (SmartLipo)
  • Recovery: 1-2 weeks for swelling/bruising, 4-6 weeks for final results
  • Stay required: 7-10 days minimum in Beijing
  • Best time to visit: Spring (March-May) or Autumn (September-November) for comfortable recovery weather

✨ Other Popular Procedures

Double Eyelid Surgery (Asian Blepharoplasty)

Creates a visible eyelid crease. Techniques include incisional method (permanent), suture method (less invasive), and partial incision. Recovery: 1-2 weeks initial, 3-6 months final results. Shanghai Ninth People's Hospital is the global leader in this procedure.

Price: $1,500 - $3,000

Rhinoplasty (Nose Reshaping)

Reshapes nose for improved aesthetics and function. Types include augmentation, reduction, and revision surgery. Chinese surgeons excel at Asian rhinoplasty, which requires different techniques than Western noses. Recovery: 1-2 weeks social activities, 6-12 months final shape.

Price: $3,000 - $6,000

🔍 How to Choose a Plastic Surgery Hospital in China

5 Key Criteria for Selection

  1. Hospital Accreditation: Look for Grade 3A (三级甲等) public hospitals or JCI-accredited private hospitals
  2. Surgeon Credentials: Board certification, years of experience, before/after photos, international training
  3. Communication: Ensure English-speaking staff or arrange a medical interpreter
  4. Facility Standards: Modern operating rooms, anesthesia safety protocols, emergency capabilities
  5. Aftercare: Post-operative follow-up plan, revision policy, and international patient support

⚠️ Red Flags to Avoid

  • Prices that seem "too good to be true" (significantly below market rates)
  • Surgeons who cannot provide board certification or licensing documentation
  • Clinics that pressure you to book immediately without proper consultation
  • Facilities that don't allow you to meet your surgeon before surgery day
  • No clear post-operative care plan or emergency contact
