China International Medical Tourism Expo 2026: What to Expect This April

📅 April 3, 2026 📝 China Hospitals Guide
The China International Medical Tourism Expo returns to Shanghai in April 2026 — bringing together hospitals, wellness providers, and industry leaders to showcase China's growing capabilities as a medical tourism destination.

📍 China International Medical Tourism Expo 2026

📅 Date: April 23-25, 2026

📍 Venue: Shanghai Exhibition Center

This major industry event will explore evolving trends in medical tourism and functional medicine — covering the full health journey from prevention to treatment to rehabilitation.

🔍 What to Expect at the Expo

The exhibition will bring together a wide range of medical tourism stakeholders:

  • International medical institutions — top-tier Chinese hospitals with international departments
  • Specialized hospitals — cancer centers, cardiac hospitals, fertility clinics, orthopedics centers
  • Wellness centers — TCM wellness, rehabilitation, preventive health screening
  • Medical tourism agencies — coordinators, facilitators, and service providers
  • Technology providers — medical equipment, telemedicine platforms, translation services

📊 Medical Tourism to China: Still Emerging but Growing

A recent analysis from MedicalTourismWatch.com notes that while China is seeing increased foreign patient visits at certain facilities, medical tourism to China remains an emerging corridor rather than a mass market — at least for now.

According to Caixin Global (February 2026), major international departments in Chinese hospitals are reporting growth in foreign patient numbers. However, industry observers note that China has yet to develop the comprehensive "medical + tourism" infrastructure that countries like Thailand and Malaysia have perfected.

🎯 What This Means for International Patients

The combination of government policy support (nine ministries), growing industry events, and expanding TCM wellness offerings signals that China is serious about developing its medical tourism sector.

For international patients considering treatment in China, this Expo represents an opportunity to:

  • Meet hospital representatives directly
  • Learn about latest treatment technologies and costs
  • Understand the coordination services available
  • Explore combined medical + wellness trip options
