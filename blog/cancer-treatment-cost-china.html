Limitations & Considerations

⚠️ Important Limitations to Consider

Drug Access Limitations

  • Some Western Drugs Unavailable: Certain FDA-approved drugs may not yet be available in China. However, Chinese alternatives or domestic clinical trials may be options.
  • Drug Approval Timelines: While China has accelerated approvals, some newer therapies may have a 1-3 year lag compared to US FDA approval.
  • Clinical Trial Access: China offers unique clinical trial opportunities unavailable elsewhere, but participation requires meeting specific criteria.

Healthcare System Considerations

  • Documentation: Medical records must be translated; some hospitals require apostilled documents
  • Follow-up Care: Long-term follow-up typically requires local oncologist coordination in your home country
  • Travel Requirements: Medical visas (M visa) are required; plan 2-4 weeks for documentation processing
  • Insurance: Verify coverage with your insurer before traveling; some international plans cover overseas treatment

Quality Assurance

  • Hospital Selection: Stick to top-tier hospitals with international patient experience
  • Communication: Major hospitals have English staff, but complex medical discussions may require professional medical interpreters
  • continuity: Request complete medical records in English for seamless care transition home
