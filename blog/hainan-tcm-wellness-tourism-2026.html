❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is TCM wellness evidence-based?

A: Yes. Many TCM treatments have been validated by modern clinical research. Acupuncture, for example, is now recognized by WHO and covered by many international health insurance plans for certain conditions.

Q: Can I combine TCM with my existing Western medical treatment?

A: Absolutely. Many patients come to Hainan specifically for integrated treatment plans that combine Western medicine with TCM approaches. We recommend discussing this with both your current doctors and our TCM specialists.

Q: What should I expect during a TCM consultation?

A: A typical TCM consultation includes: pulse diagnosis (把脉), tongue examination (舌诊), observation of complexion, detailed health history, and body constitution analysis (体质辨识). Plan for 30-60 minutes with a senior TCM practitioner.

Q: Is Hainan safe for international tourists?

A: Hainan is one of China's safest provinces for tourists. International-standard hospitals, tourist police, and English-speaking staff at major medical facilities ensure a comfortable experience.
