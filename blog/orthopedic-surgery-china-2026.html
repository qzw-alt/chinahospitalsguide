Types of Orthopedic Procedures Available

Joint Replacement Surgery

  • Total Knee Replacement (TKR): For severe arthritis or joint damage
  • Total Hip Replacement (THR): For hip arthritis, fractures, or avascular necrosis
  • Shoulder Replacement: For severe shoulder arthritis or fractures
  • Revision Joint Surgery: Replacement of failed previous joint replacements

Spine Surgery

  • Spinal Fusion: For degenerative disc disease, spondylolisthesis
  • Disc Replacement: Artificial disc replacement for herniated discs
  • Laminectomy: Decompression surgery for spinal stenosis
  • Minimally Invasive Spine Surgery: Smaller incisions, faster recovery

Sports Medicine

  • ACL Reconstruction: Knee ligament repair using arthroscopy
  • Meniscus Repair: Arthroscopic meniscus surgery
  • Rotator Cuff Repair: Shoulder tendon repair

The Treatment Process: What to Expect

Before Surgery

  1. Initial Consultation: Video consultation with your surgeon
  2. Medical Records Review: Send X-rays, MRIs, and medical history
  3. Treatment Plan: Receive detailed surgical plan and cost estimate
  4. Travel Arrangements: Coordinate surgery date and book flights

Typical Timeline for Joint Replacement

  • Day 1: Arrival, hospital admission, pre-operative tests
  • Day 2: Surgery day
  • Day 3-5: Post-operative care, pain management, physiotherapy
  • Day 6-10: Intensive physiotherapy, discharge preparation
  • Day 11-14: Final check-up, travel home
Important: Plan to stay in China for 2-3 weeks for joint replacement surgery. This allows adequate time for initial recovery and physiotherapy before flying home.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is orthopedic surgery in China safe?

Yes. Top Chinese orthopedic hospitals are JCI-accredited and meet international standards. Many surgeons have trained at Western institutions, and hospitals use the same implants and equipment as US and European facilities.

Will language be a problem?

No. International departments provide English-speaking coordinators who assist throughout your stay. Many doctors also speak English. For $299 Standard Package, we include translation services.

What implants are used?

Chinese hospitals use the same implant brands as Western hospitals (Zimmer, Stryker, DePuy). You can request specific brands if you have a preference.

What about aftercare when I return home?

Your surgeon will provide detailed post-operative instructions and be available for video consultations. We recommend arranging follow-up care with a local orthopedic surgeon before you travel.
