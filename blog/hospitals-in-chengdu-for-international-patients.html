Why International Patients Choose Chengdu

Chengdu is the medical hub of Southwest China, serving a catchment area of roughly 200 million people across Sichuan, Yunnan, Guizhou, Qinghai, and Tibet. The city's flagship institution, West China Hospital (四川大学华西医院), consistently ranks among the top 3 hospitals in all of China — alongside Peking Union Medical College Hospital in Beijing and Ruijin in Shanghai.

For international patients, Chengdu offers several advantages: significantly lower costs than equivalent treatment in the US or Europe, some of China's most specialized medical teams, shorter wait times for complex procedures, and growing English-language patient services. The city also has a direct international airport with connections to major Asian hubs, making it accessible from Southeast Asia, the Middle East, and beyond.

Who typically comes to Chengdu? Patients seeking complex oncology treatment (particularly gastric, esophageal, and lung cancers, which are more prevalent in Southwest China), spinal surgery, cardiac procedures, and fertility treatment. Chengdu also attracts patients from neighboring countries where specialized care is limited.

West China Hospital — The National Powerhouse

West China Hospital (四川大学华西医院)

Ranked Top 3 Nationally 3,000+ Beds International Medical Center Oncology · Neurology · Orthopedics English-Speaking Coordinators

West China Hospital is the flagship teaching hospital of Sichuan University and one of the most respected medical institutions in Asia. Founded in 1892 as a missionary hospital, it has grown into a 4-campus medical complex handling over 5 million outpatient visits annually. Its cancer center is one of the largest in Asia, and its neurosurgery and orthopedics departments draw complex referral cases from across the region.

International services: The hospital operates a dedicated International Medical Center (国际医疗中心) in Building 1, providing appointment scheduling, interpreter services, and case management for foreign patients. Staff coordinators speak English, Korean, Japanese, and Arabic.

Address: No. 37 Guoxue Lane, Wuhou District, Chengdu (武侯区国学巷37号). Metro Line 1 (Huaxiba stop) or Line 3 (Gaopeng Avenue stop).

Other Top Hospitals in Chengdu

Sichuan Provincial People's Hospital (四川省人民医院)

Large General Hospital VIP / International Ward Cardiology · General Surgery Accepts International Insurance

The provincial flagship general hospital with strong departments in cardiology, emergency medicine, and general surgery. Its VIP international ward provides English-language consultations and accepts most major international insurance plans. A good option when West China's wait times are long for non-complex cases.

Address: No. 32 Wuhouci Street, Wuhou District, Chengdu.

Chengdu Women and Children's Central Hospital (成都市妇幼保健院)

Obstetrics · Gynecology · Pediatrics High-Risk Pregnancy NICU

The premier facility for maternal and child health in Southwest China. Handles everything from routine prenatal care to high-risk pregnancies and complex neonatal cases. Popular with expat families living in Chengdu for obstetrics and pediatric specialty care.

Address: No. 161 Second Section, Renmin Road South, Chengdu.

Sichuan Provincial Cancer Hospital (四川省肿瘤医院)

Dedicated Cancer Center Radiation Oncology Chemotherapy Surgical Oncology

Sichuan's only specialized cancer hospital, treating approximately 50,000 new cancer cases annually. Particularly strong in gastrointestinal cancers, lung cancer, and radiation therapy. Draws patients from across Southwest China who need focused oncology care without the general hospital environment.

Address: No. 42 Third Section, West Yihuan Road, Chengdu.

Chengdu University of TCM / Sichuan Provincial TCM Hospital (成都中医药大学附属医院)

Integrative Medicine TCM · Acupuncture Rehabilitation

For patients interested in combining Western medicine with Traditional Chinese Medicine, this teaching hospital offers acupuncture, herbal medicine, and rehabilitation services alongside conventional treatment. Commonly used for post-surgical recovery, chronic pain management, and wellness programs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does West China Hospital accept foreign patients?

Yes. West China Hospital actively serves international patients through its International Medical Center. The hospital accepts international insurance (check with your provider for direct billing arrangements), provides English-speaking coordinators, and handles medical record translations.

What is the best hospital in Chengdu for complex surgery?

West China Hospital is the top choice for complex cases — it ranks among the top 3 nationally for oncology, neurology, orthopedics, and cardiac surgery. For cardiac procedures specifically, both West China and Sichuan Provincial People's Hospital have excellent cardiac surgery departments.

Can I get English-speaking doctors in Chengdu?

Yes, particularly at West China Hospital's International Medical Center, Chengdu United Family Hospital, and the VIP wards of Sichuan Provincial People's Hospital. For routine outpatient care, English ability varies. For complex inpatient treatment, the international desk will arrange dedicated English-speaking medical teams.

How much does surgery cost at West China compared to the US?

Surgery at West China Hospital typically costs 60-80% less than equivalent procedures in the US. A hip replacement runs approximately $8,000–$12,000 USD versus $35,000–$50,000 in the US. Cardiac bypass surgery costs $12,000–$18,000 at West China versus $80,000–$120,000 in the US.

Do I need a visa for medical treatment in Chengdu?

Most nationalities can obtain a medical stay visa (M visa) or use the visa-free transit policy (currently up to 240 hours at Chengdu airport for select nationalities). For longer treatment plans, the hospital's international center can provide a medical invitation letter for M visa application. Contact us for assistance with the visa process.

Is Chengdu safe for foreign patients?

Chengdu is one of China's safest major cities. West China Hospital's area (Wuhou District) is well-developed, with hotels, restaurants, and international amenities nearby. The hospital itself has a reputation for handling international patients professionally and respectfully.
