Top 10 Wellness Cities in China 2026: Where TCM, Hot Springs & Medical Tourism Meet

Quick Answer: China's wellness tourism is booming — from Hainan's TCM retreats to Chengdu's hot springs and Hangzhou's tea culture spas. This guide ranks the top 10 Chinese cities for wellness travelers in 2026, covering traditional therapies, modern medical wellness, costs, and visa-free access.

1. Hainan (Sanya & Boao) — China's Wellness Capital

Best for: TCM wellness retreats, medical tourism, tropical recovery

Hainan leads China's wellness scene with the Boao Lecheng Medical Tourism Pilot Zone, offering cutting-edge treatments with visa-free access for 59 countries. Combine TCM acupuncture, herbal baths, and beach recovery in Sanya's 5-star wellness resorts.

Key attractions: Boao Lecheng international hospitals, Sanya TCM wellness resorts, tropical hot springs

Read more: Hainan TCM Wellness Tourism Guide →

2. Hangzhou — Tea Culture & Lakeside Healing

Best for: Tea therapy, meditation retreats, lakeside wellness

Home to Longjing (Dragon Well) tea, Hangzhou offers unique tea-based spa treatments and Buddhist meditation retreats around West Lake. The city's serene temples and tea plantations create the perfect wellness escape.

3. Chengdu — Hot Springs & Sichuan TCM

Best for: Natural hot springs, Sichuan herbal medicine, panda therapy experiences

Chengdu surrounds itself with natural hot spring resorts in the Qingcheng Mountains. Combined with Sichuan's unique TCM traditions and the famous slow-paced lifestyle, it's an ideal wellness destination.

Read more: Chengdu Hospitals for International Patients →

4. Kunming — The Spring City's Healing Climate

Best for: Respiratory health, year-round mild climate wellness

Kunming's nickname "Spring City" comes from its perfect year-round climate (15-25°C). It's a top destination for respiratory health retreats, with clean mountain air and Yunnan's rich herbal medicine traditions.

5. Beijing — Imperial TCM & Modern Medical Wellness

Best for: Traditional Chinese medicine hospitals, imperial wellness traditions

Beijing offers China's most prestigious TCM hospitals alongside modern wellness centers. Visit Tongrentang (350-year-old pharmacy), try imperial TCM treatments, or book executive health screenings at PUMCH.

Read more: Beijing Hospitals for International Patients →

6. Guilin — Nature Immersion & Tai Chi

Best for: Nature therapy, tai chi, landscape healing

Guilin's iconic karst mountains provide the ultimate backdrop for wellness. Join riverside tai chi sessions, bamboo forest qigong, and meditation retreats surrounded by some of China's most stunning scenery.

7. Shanghai — Luxury Medical Wellness Hub

Best for: Premium health screening, international hospital wellness packages

Shanghai leads in premium medical wellness with JCI-accredited international hospitals offering executive health screening packages. Combine a full-body checkup with luxury spa treatments and French Concession wellness cafes.

Read more: Shanghai Hospitals for International Patients →

8. Lijiang — High-Altitude Healing & Naxi Traditions

Best for: High-altitude wellness, ethnic Naxi herbal medicine

At 2,400m altitude, Lijiang offers unique high-altitude wellness experiences. The Naxi ethnic minority's Dongba herbal medicine traditions and Jade Dragon Snow Mountain's pristine environment make it a unique healing destination.

9. Xiamen — Coastal Wellness & Tea Culture

Best for: Coastal wellness, oolong tea culture, island therapy

Xiamen's Gulangyu Island (UNESCO World Heritage) is a car-free wellness sanctuary. The city's Fujian oolong tea culture, coastal walking paths, and mild climate make it perfect for a relaxing wellness break.

10. Huangshan — Mountain Qigong & Hot Springs

Best for: Mountain qigong, natural hot springs, landscape meditation

Practice qigong atop the legendary Yellow Mountains, soak in 1,000-year-old hot springs, and experience Taoist wellness traditions in one of China's most breathtaking settings.

🌿 TCM Wellness & Health Screening in China

Premium health checkups in China often include a TCM constitution assessment (中医体质辨识) alongside Western diagnostics:

  • TCM constitution typing — categorizes patients into 9 body types (qi-deficiency, yang-deficiency, damp-heat, etc.) to guide lifestyle and dietary advice.
  • Preventive TCM therapies — acupuncture, moxibustion, or herbal tonics prescribed based on constitution.
