Breaking Down the Costs

Public Hospitals vs Private Clinics

Public Hospitals (Recommended for value):

  • IVF Cycle: ¥25,000-50,000 CNY ($3,500-7,000 USD)
  • Medications: ¥8,000-15,000 CNY ($1,100-2,100 USD)
  • Total First Cycle: ¥35,000-70,000 CNY ($4,900-9,800 USD)

Private Clinics (Better for international patients):

  • IVF Cycle: ¥40,000-80,000 CNY ($5,500-11,000 USD)
  • Medications: ¥10,000-20,000 CNY ($1,400-2,800 USD)
  • Total First Cycle: ¥55,000-110,000 CNY ($7,700-15,400 USD)

What's Included?

  • Initial consultation & fertility testing
  • Ovarian stimulation monitoring
  • Egg retrieval procedure
  • Laboratory fertilization (IVF or ICSI)
  • Embryo transfer
  • First pregnancy test

Additional Costs to Budget:

  • ICSI (if needed): $560-1,100
  • Embryo freezing (per year): $170-490
  • Frozen embryo transfer: $840-2,100
  • PGT-A genetic screening: $2,000-4,000

Top 5 IVF Clinics in China

1. Shanghai Ji Ai Genetics & IVF Institute (上海集爱)

  • Success Rate: 50-60% (under 35)
  • Cost: ¥45,000-75,000 CNY per cycle
  • Why Choose: Best English support, partnership with US Genetics & IVF Institute, international patient services
  • Best For: Foreign couples, English speakers

2. Peking University Third Hospital (PUTH) (北医三院)

  • Success Rate: 50-55% (under 35)
  • Cost: ¥30,000-45,000 CNY per cycle
  • Why Choose: China's most famous fertility center—first IVF baby in mainland China born here in 1988
  • Best For: Value seekers, those with Chinese language support
  • Note: Long wait times (3-6 months)

3. CITIC-Xiangya Reproductive Hospital (中信湘雅)

  • Success Rate: 50-58% (under 35)
  • Cost: ¥35,000-55,000 CNY per cycle
  • Why Choose: China's largest fertility hospital by volume, pioneered many IVF techniques
  • Best For: Complex cases, experienced with international patients

4. Shanghai First Maternity and Infant Hospital

  • Success Rate: 45-50% (under 35)
  • Cost: ¥30,000-48,000 CNY per cycle
  • Why Choose: Large public hospital, good results at lower cost
  • Best For: Budget-conscious couples in Shanghai

5. Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hospital (中山大学附属第二医院)

  • Success Rate: 48-55% (under 35)
  • Cost: ¥32,000-50,000 CNY per cycle
  • Why Choose: Leading southern China center, strong research program
  • Best For: Couples in Guangzhou/Shenzhen area

Important Legal Requirements

Who Can Access IVF in China?

✓ Eligible:

  • Married heterosexual couples
  • Foreign couples with valid marriage certificates
  • Women under 49 years old (varies by clinic)
  • Couples with proven infertility (typically 1+ year trying)

✗ Not Eligible:

  • Single women
  • Unmarried couples
  • Same-sex couples
  • Anyone seeking surrogacy (illegal in China)

Required Documentation:

  1. Marriage certificate (original + notarized Chinese translation)
  2. Passports (both partners)
  3. Medical records proving infertility
  4. Health clearances (HIV, hepatitis B/C, syphilis screening)

Insurance Coverage Update 2026

Good news: Beijing now includes IVF in public health insurance, covering up to 3 cycles for eligible couples. Several other major cities are piloting similar programs.

Coverage typically includes:

  • Consultation and monitoring
  • Egg retrieval and embryo transfer
  • Basic medications

Not covered:

  • ICSI (additional $560-1,100)
  • Genetic screening (PGT-A)
  • Embryo freezing fees

Is China Right for Your IVF Journey?

Choose China If:

  • ✅ You're a married couple seeking significant cost savings
  • ✅ You can access top-tier clinics in Beijing/Shanghai/Guangzhou
  • ✅ You're comfortable navigating language/cultural differences
  • ✅ You want world-class technology at affordable prices

Consider Alternatives If:

  • ❌ You're single or unmarried
  • ❌ You need extensive English support (Thailand may be better)
  • ❌ You're seeking surrogacy or gender selection (illegal in China)
  • ❌ You prefer established medical tourism infrastructure

Getting Started: Next Steps

  1. Gather documentation - Get your marriage certificate notarized and translated
  2. Choose your clinic - Contact 2-3 clinics for consultations and quotes
  3. Plan your stay - Budget 4-6 weeks in China for a full cycle
  4. Consider a facilitator - Medical tourism agencies can help with logistics

China offers world-class IVF treatment at 60-75% less than Western costs, with top clinics achieving 50-60% success rates for women under 35. While legal restrictions and language barriers present challenges, the cost savings and clinical excellence make it an attractive option for eligible couples.
