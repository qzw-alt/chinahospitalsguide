Jinan Respiratory Hospital Rankings 2026: Top Facilities for Pulmonology & Respiratory Care

Jinan, capital of Shandong Province, hosts some of northern China's most respected respiratory and pulmonology departments. This guide ranks the top hospitals for lung conditions including lung cancer, COPD, asthma, and respiratory infections — with 2026 cost estimates and international patient information.

Why International Patients Choose Jinan for Respiratory Care

Jinan is home to Shandong Province's most advanced medical institutions, including two hospitals affiliated with Shandong University — one of China's top-10 universities. The concentration of academic medical centers gives Jinan's respiratory departments an edge in complex cases: lung cancer surgery, interventional pulmonology, and targeted therapy programs.

Key finding: Qilu Hospital's Department of Respiratory and Critical Care Medicine has been ranked among China's top 10 pulmonology departments for 6 consecutive years, with published outcomes data for lung cancer surgery and bronchoscopic interventions.

Lung Cancer Treatment in Jinan: What International Patients Should Know

Jinan's top hospitals have adopted international treatment protocols for lung cancer, including molecular genomic testing (EGFR, ALK, ROS1, KRAS, BRAF), targeted therapy access, immunotherapy programs (PD-1/PD-L1 inhibitors), minimally invasive VATS surgery, and multidisciplinary tumor boards.

How to Arrange Respiratory Treatment in Jinan

  1. Submit medical records — Pathology reports, CT/MRI scans, and prior treatment history
  2. Receive treatment plan and cost estimate — Typically within 24-72 hours
  3. Travel and arrival — Jinan is accessible via Jinan Yaoqiang International Airport (TNA)
  4. Multidisciplinary evaluation — 1-2 days of consultations and pre-treatment testing
  5. Treatment initiation — Surgery or targeted/immunotherapy begins according to agreed plan

Frequently Asked Questions

Which is the best respiratory hospital in Jinan for international patients?

Qilu Hospital of Shandong University is the top respiratory hospital in Jinan, with nationally recognized pulmonology and thoracic surgery departments.

How much does lung cancer treatment cost in Jinan?

Lung cancer lobectomy typically costs $8,000-$15,000 USD; targeted therapy drugs $2,000-$6,000 per month. Compared to US costs of $150,000+ for comprehensive treatment, Jinan offers 70-85% savings.

Can international patients access targeted therapy drugs in Jinan?

Yes. Jinan's top hospitals have access to most internationally-approved targeted therapy drugs at prices 60-80% lower than in Western countries, including Osimertinib, Gefitinib, and immunotherapy agents.
