Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really free? How do you make money?

Yes, our service is completely free for patients. We partner with JCI-certified hospitals in China, and they cover our coordination fees. You pay only for the medical treatment directly to the hospital.

How do I know the hospitals are qualified?

We work only with hospitals that meet international standards. Many of our partner hospitals are JCI (Joint Commission International) accredited, and all have English-speaking staff and dedicated international patient departments.

What if I don't speak Chinese?

No problem! We provide medical interpreters at all major hospitals. Our coordinators also communicate with you in English throughout the entire process.

How long does the process take?

Most inquiries get a response within 24 hours. Once you approve a treatment plan, appointments can typically be scheduled within 1-2 weeks. Visa processing and travel arrangements add another 2-4 weeks.

What if something goes wrong?

We have 24/7 support available throughout your treatment. If any issues arise, our coordinators act as your advocate, working directly with the hospital administration to resolve them.

Can I get a second opinion?

Absolutely. We can help you get second opinions from multiple specialists before you make any decisions. This is part of our free consultation service.
