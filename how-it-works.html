Frequently Asked Questions

How are you different from other medical-travel services?

Most medical-tourism agencies earn commissions from hospitals. We do not. We charge a clear coordination fee and have no financial relationship with any hospital. That means you get an honest, unbiased read on the options that fit your case.

Do I pay the hospital directly?

Yes. Hospital treatment costs are paid directly to the hospital — just like at home. We do not handle hospital payments or collect on the hospital's behalf.

What if I don't speak Chinese?

For pre-arrival coordination — record translation, appointment messages, and hospital-side communication before you arrive — we handle the English-Chinese bridge. In-stay interpretation at the hospital is arranged by the hospital itself, not by us.

Can I choose any hospital I want?

Yes. We provide a shortlist based on your case. You make the final choice. Once you decide, we coordinate the appointment with that hospital.

Is the coordination fee refundable?

Starter Guide ($30): non-refundable once delivery work begins. Standard Package ($299): $269 refunded if you cancel 72+ hours before service start; the first $30 is non-refundable. Full refund terms are on the pricing page.

What happens once I arrive at the hospital?

That's where our scope ends. The hospital takes over for treatment, in-stay interpretation, and stay logistics. We don't replace insurance, hospital concierge, or post-care follow-up — those sit with the provider or with a separate arrangement.

Which hospitals do you coordinate with?

We coordinate with major hospitals across China — public tertiary hospitals, specialty centers, and international-standard facilities. We match you to the most appropriate hospital for your specific condition, not the hospital that pays us.
