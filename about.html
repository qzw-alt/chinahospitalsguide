About China Hospitals Guide

Bridging the gap between international patients and world-class Chinese healthcare since 2026

Our Story

China Hospitals Guide was founded in 2026 with a simple mission: to help international patients access the exceptional healthcare that China has to offer at a fraction of the cost they would pay in their home countries.

Our founder, after witnessing friends and family struggle with exorbitant medical costs and long waiting lists in the US and Europe, recognized an opportunity. China has some of the world's best hospitals, highly skilled doctors, and advanced medical technology.

We set out to change that. By combining deep knowledge of China's healthcare system with English-language support and personalized guidance, we've helped hundreds of patients from over 30 countries receive life-changing medical treatment in China.
