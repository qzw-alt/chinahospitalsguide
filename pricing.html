What happens after payment

This is the part many service websites forget. Patients want to know the handoff after checkout, especially for a medical travel service.

1. Confirmation

Once payment is complete, you move to contact and case intake so we can confirm your priorities and records.

2. Case materials

You share the details we need for the chosen package, such as medical history, reports, and timing.

3. Service begins

We start the research or coordination work that matches the package you purchased.

Already paid?

Go directly to the contact page or consultation form and mention that payment is complete. That gives us a clean handoff from checkout into service delivery.

How to decide between the two packages

Choose Starter Guide if

  • You are still researching hospitals or cities
  • You want structure before committing to hands-on support
  • You are comfortable doing more follow-through yourself

Choose Standard Package if

  • You are ready to move from planning into action
  • You need help with records and appointment setup
  • You want a clearer handoff into pre-arrival coordination

Use free case review first if

  • You are not sure whether China is the right option yet
  • You want help selecting the right package
  • You need a human answer before paying
