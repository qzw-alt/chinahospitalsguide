❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I don't speak Chinese. How do I communicate at the hospital?

Top Chinese hospitals have bilingual coordinators in their international departments. Many senior doctors have overseas training and speak English. Translation apps can help with day-to-day needs.

Q: How do the costs compare to my home country?

See the price comparison section above. In general, patients save 60-80% compared to US/UK prices for equivalent quality at top-tier hospitals.

Q: What if I have complications after returning home?

You receive a bilingual medical summary at discharge. Our service includes remote follow-up coordination so you can stay in touch with your doctor.

Q: Can I pay with credit card?

Most international departments accept Visa/Mastercard. We also recommend downloading Alipay and binding your international card before arrival — China is largely cashless.

Q: Can my family come with me?

Yes. 1-2 family members can accompany you. Hotels and short-term apartments are available near all major hospitals.
