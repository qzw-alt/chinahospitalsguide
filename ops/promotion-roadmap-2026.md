# Promotion Roadmap for China Hospitals Guide

Updated: 2026-04-29

## Positioning

Primary angle: practical English-language guidance for foreigners, expats, students, travelers, HR teams, and international patients who need to understand hospitals in China.

Main trust promise: clear hospital navigation, preparation checklists, cost context, and coordination support. Avoid promising medical outcomes.

## First 30 Days

1. Publish and internally link the core guide pages:
   - `/blog/foreigners-guide-healthcare-china.html`
   - `/medical-chinese-phrases.html`
   - `/hospitals.html`
   - `/checklist.html`
   - `/contact-new.html`

2. Submit updated sitemap:
   - Google Search Console
   - Bing Webmaster Tools

3. Share the guide in non-spammy formats:
   - Reddit: r/chinalife, r/shanghai, r/beijing
   - Facebook expat groups for Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Shenzhen, Chengdu
   - LinkedIn posts for HR, relocation, insurance, and international school audiences

4. Build link outreach list:
   - relocation agencies
   - international schools
   - expat blogs
   - insurance brokers
   - university international student offices
   - HR newsletters

https://chinahospitalsguide.com/blog/foreigners-guide-healthcare-china.html

It may be useful for new arrivals, students, foreign employees, or families in China. If you maintain a resources page or send helpful links to your community, feel free to include it.

Best,
China Hospitals Guide
