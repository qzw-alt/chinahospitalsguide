# Status cheat sheet

Use this as the simplest rule set while you are still operating manually.

## New visitor, not paid

- `payment_status`: `Not paid yet`
- `lead_status`: `New lead`
- `follow_up_stage`: `Awaiting review`

## You replied and asked a question

- `payment_status`: `Not paid yet`
- `lead_status`: `Waiting for reply`
- `follow_up_stage`: `Awaiting package recommendation`

## Starter paid

- `payment_status`: `Paid - starter`
- `lead_status`: `Paid client`
- `follow_up_stage`: `Awaiting review`

## Standard paid

- `payment_status`: `Paid - standard`
- `lead_status`: `Paid client`
- `follow_up_stage`: `Awaiting records`

## Records received

- `lead_status`: `In progress`
- `follow_up_stage`: `Awaiting appointment step`

## Near handoff / travel prep

- `lead_status`: `In progress`
- `follow_up_stage`: `Awaiting travel planning`

## Finished

- `lead_status`: `Closed`
- `follow_up_stage`: `Completed`
