Compare joint replacement, spine surgery, sports medicine & orthopedic care at China's top-ranked hospitals. Save 60-80% vs Western prices.

Why Choose China for Orthopedic Surgery?

China has emerged as a leading destination for orthopedic surgery, offering internationally trained surgeons, advanced surgical technology, and costs 60-80% lower than the USA or UK. Major Chinese orthopedic hospitals perform thousands of procedures annually with outcomes comparable to top Western institutions.

  • Cost savings: Joint replacement in China costs $8,000-$18,000 vs $40,000-$60,000 in the USA
  • Experienced surgeons: Top Chinese orthopedic surgeons train internationally and perform high volumes of procedures
  • Modern facilities: Many hospitals use the same implant systems (Zimmer, Stryker, DePuy) as Western hospitals
  • Short wait times: Most procedures can be scheduled within 2-4 weeks

Top Orthopedic Hospitals in China

Beijing

  • Jishuitan Hospital (积水潭医院) — #1 Orthopedics in China. Specializes in joint replacement, trauma, spine, and hand surgery. International patient services available.
  • Peking University Third Hospital (北医三院) — Top orthopedics and sports medicine center. Strong in knee/hip replacement and arthroscopic surgery.
  • PLA General Hospital (301 Hospital) — Comprehensive orthopedic surgery across all subspecialties.

Shanghai

  • Shanghai Sixth People's Hospital (上海六院) — #2 Orthopedics in China. Known for microsurgery, limb reconstruction, and joint replacement.
  • Changzheng Hospital (长征医院) — Top spine surgery center in Shanghai.

Other Key Cities

  • Fuzhou: Fujian Medical University Union Hospital, Fujian Provincial Hospital — See full Fuzhou orthopedic rankings
  • Guangzhou: Southern Hospital, Nanfang Hospital — strong in joint replacement and sports medicine
  • Chengdu: West China Hospital — top orthopedic department in Southwest China

What to Expect: The Orthopedic Surgery Journey

Before You Travel

  1. Send your medical records and imaging (X-rays, MRI, CT) for preliminary review
  2. We help match you with 3-5 suitable hospitals based on your specific condition
  3. Hospital confirms the treatment plan and cost estimate
  4. Arrange medical visa (M visa) and travel

During Your Stay

  1. Pre-operative consultation and final imaging (1-2 days)
  2. Surgery (1 day for most procedures)
  3. Hospital recovery (3-7 days for joint replacement, 5-10 days for spine)
  4. Post-operative physiotherapy begins before discharge

Recovery & Follow-up

Most patients can return home 2-3 weeks after joint replacement surgery. Your surgeon will provide a detailed recovery plan, and we can help arrange follow-up communication with the hospital.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are the implants used in China the same as in Western countries?

Yes. Top Chinese hospitals use internationally certified implant systems from Johnson & Johnson (DePuy), Zimmer Biomet, Stryker, and Smith & Nephew — the same brands used in the USA and Europe.

Do Chinese orthopedic surgeons speak English?

Many orthopedic surgeons at major teaching hospitals in Beijing and Shanghai speak English. However, we recommend working with a medical coordinator for seamless communication with hospital staff.

How long is the recovery before I can fly home?

For joint replacement, most patients are cleared to fly 10-14 days after surgery. For spine surgery, plan for 2-3 weeks before flying. Your surgeon will provide specific clearance based on your recovery.

What about post-operative care after I return home?

Your Chinese surgeon will provide a complete discharge summary and post-operative care instructions in English. You can follow up with a local orthopedic surgeon for continued physiotherapy and monitoring.
