Patients typically save 50-70% on stem cell therapy in China. A typical treatment course costs $15,000-$40,000 in China vs $40,000-$100,000+ in the US or Europe. The exact savings depend on the type of treatment, number of injections, cell source, and hospital selected. Complex treatments like Parkinson's or spinal cord injury show the highest savings potential.
Chinese stem cell clinics primarily use mesenchymal stem cells (MSCs) derived from umbilical cord tissue, adipose tissue, or bone marrow. Umbilical cord-derived MSCs are most common due to their potency, availability, and lower rejection risk. Neural stem cells may be used for neurological conditions. Treatment protocols specify cell type, dose (typically 50-200 million cells), and delivery method (IV, intrathecal, or local injection).
China has established regulations for stem cell therapy through the National Health Commission. Top hospitals we recommend are JCI-accredited, follow strict protocols, and have published clinical research. While not all treatments are approved by the FDA or EMA, many patients report positive outcomes. We help verify clinic credentials, review treatment approaches, and ensure hospitals meet international standards before making recommendations.

This includes initial evaluation (1-2 days), treatment sessions (typically 1-3 injections spaced a few days apart), and monitoring period (3-7 days post-treatment). Some conditions may require multiple visits over several months for optimal results. We arrange all travel logistics and provide visa invitation letters for medical travel.
