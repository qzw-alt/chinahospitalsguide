China has emerged as a global leader in oncology care, with several hospitals ranking among the world's best for cancer treatment. Chinese oncology centers offer the latest therapies—including CAR-T cell therapy, proton therapy, immunotherapy, and precision medicine—at a fraction of US prices.

Treatment Options Available

🧬 Immunotherapy & Targeted Therapy

PD-1/PD-L1 inhibitors (pembrolizumab, nivolumab, toripalimab) and CAR-T cell therapy. China approved the world's first CAR-T therapy and has multiple approved products.

⚛️ Proton & Heavy Ion Therapy

Precision radiation with minimal damage to surrounding tissue. Available at Shanghai Proton & Heavy Ion Hospital and several other centers across China.

🔪 Surgical Oncology

Robotic-assisted surgery, minimally invasive procedures, and complex tumor resections performed by world-class surgeons.

💊 Chemotherapy & Combination Therapy

Latest chemotherapy protocols, often combined with immunotherapy or targeted therapy for better outcomes.

Top Cancer Hospitals in China

🏥 Fudan University Shanghai Cancer Center

Top-ranked oncology hospital in China. Specializes in breast cancer, lymphoma, gastrointestinal cancers, and precision medicine.

🏥 Sun Yat-sen University Cancer Center

Leading center in southern China, particularly strong in nasopharyngeal carcinoma, liver cancer, and colorectal cancer.

🏥 Beijing Cancer Hospital

Premier oncology institution in northern China with expertise in lung, gastric, and esophageal cancers.

🏥 Fuda Cancer Hospital (Guangzhou)

Pioneer in CAR-T therapy and cryosurgery for cancer treatment. International patient department with English-speaking staff.
