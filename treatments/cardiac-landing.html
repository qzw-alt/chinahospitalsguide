Top Cardiac Hospitals in China

Fuwai Hospital (Beijing) — #1 Cardiology in China

World's highest-volume cardiac surgery center. Over 15,000 surgeries/year. International department with English-speaking cardiologists. Specializes in complex CABG, valve repair, TAVI, and congenital heart surgery.

Beijing Anzhen Hospital

Second-largest cardiac center in China. Strong in coronary intervention, electrophysiology, and heart transplantation. Active international patient program.

Zhongshan Hospital (Shanghai)

Fudan University-affiliated. Top-ranked cardiac surgery department in Eastern China. English-speaking international medical center.

Guangdong Provincial People's Hospital (Guangzhou)

Southern China's leading cardiac center. Strong experience with Southeast Asian international patients. Modern facilities, competitive pricing.
